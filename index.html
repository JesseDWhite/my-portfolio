About Me

I was born in Portland Oregon in 1993 and have been living here my whole life. I attended Arts and Communications Magnet Academy for highschool and graduated in 2012. After my time in highschool was over, I took a gap year to travel and gain new life experiences through AmeriCorps NCCC. This is a program that takes individuals from accross the United States and places them in teams of 10 to fulfill community service projects where need is apparent. I met my now wife of over a year in the program and have never been happier. During this time my team and I were placed at the following projects:

  • 5 weeks of disaster relief in New York City, aiding survivors of Superstorm Sandy
  • 11 weeks of camping in Agua Caliente inside the Anza Borrego desert, working to preserve six natural hot springs that serve as the water source for the desert town
  • 8 weeks of infrastructure improvements in Washington State at YMCA Camp Bishop and Huston Camp and Conference Center
  • 6 weeks working as a camp counselor for adults and children with special needs and disabilities at Easter Seals Camp Harmon
